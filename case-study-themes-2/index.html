Case Study Themes

A major component of this project was the collection of case studies around Open Education from across the globe. These case studies covered a broad range of themes that relate to open practices, uses, licensing & policy. The case studies can be accessed below and can be filtered according to themes. A theme matrix has also been developed and the case studies are accessible from there by clicking on the institutions name.
